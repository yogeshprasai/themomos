Momo Description

A typical serving of a plate of momo with sesame yellow and red garlic chilli sauce. Place of origin Nepal, Tibet, India (Northeast) Region or state South Asia Main ingredients White-flour-and-water dough; meat, vegetable or cheese filling Variations Steam-momo, Kothey momo, C-momo, Fry-momo, Open-momo It is similar to Chinese baozi and jiaozi, Mongolian buuz, Japanese gyoza and Korean mandu.
